Where dreams go to die

1. Install Metamask (this can either be with a browser extension or through mobile app): https://www.coindesk.com/learn/how-to-set-up-a-metamask-wallet/

2. In order to interact with this site on mobile you’ll need to hit this URL through the app OR via the browser that has Metamask installed

3. Purchase 31 MATIC (if you don’t have an account at an exchange get in contact with me and we can do an exchange via Paypal) — The extra 1 MATIC is for gas fees, basically the cost to use the blockchain.

4. Transfer the MATIC to your wallet address (this can be found under your Account name, which is likely ‘Account 1’)

5. You’ll now need to add the Polygon network (MATIC is the coin for the network). Click on ‘Ethereum Mainnet’ and press ‘Add Network’. Enter the details below:

  • Name: Polygon
  • New RPC URL (Choose one of the following): https://polygon-rpc.com
  • ChainID: 137
  • Symbol: MATIC
  • Explorer URL: https://polygonscan.com

6. Hard part done! You’ll see the ‘Buy-in’ button next to your name, click on that.

7. A message will pop up asking you to sign a transaction, sign that and once the transaction is settled on the blockchain you’ll get a pop-up. If you’re on mobile or have ad blockers you probably won’t get it, simply hit refresh after 30 seconds or so. If the ‘buy-in’ button is no longer by your name, you’re all set!

The new Queen/King has been crowned! What to do next?

1. Hit the ‘Get League Winner’ button. This will interact with the Smart Contract to get the winning team (which has their address/wallet tied to the name)

2. Once that transaction has settled, there will need to be THREE people to confirm that they are indeed the winner (to prevent fraud). The three people MUST be addresses/wallets that bought in and can include the person that won.

3. Once at least 3 people have confirmed the winner, the Champ can hit the ‘Claim Winnings’ button and they’ll get the pot of MATIC sent straight to their wallet!


Side (technical) note: The 'Get League Winner' button uses an Oracle (Chainlink) to hit the Premier League API which grabs the winner. This costs LINK (0.1 per request).
As there is limited LINK in the contract please only hit this button at the end of season.
It also resets the count of people who have verified the winner to zero.
