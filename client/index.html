1. Install Metamask (this can either be with a browser extension or through mobile app): https://www.coindesk.com/learn/how-to-set-up-a-metamask-wallet/

2. In order to interact with this site on mobile you’ll need to hit this URL through the app OR via the browser that has Metamask installed
3. Purchase 31 MATIC (if you don’t have an account at an exchange get in contact with me and we can do an exchange via Paypal) — The extra 1 MATIC is for gas fees, basically the cost to use the blockchain.
4. Transfer the MATIC to your wallet address (this can be found under your Account name, which is likely ‘Account 1’)
5. You’ll now need to add the Polygon network (MATIC is the coin for the network). Click on ‘Ethereum Mainnet’ and press ‘Add Network’. Enter the details below:
  • Network Name: Polygon
  • New RPC URL (Choose one of the following): https://polygon-rpc.com
  • ChainID: 137
  • Symbol: MATIC
  • Explorer URL: https://polygonscan.com
6. Hard part done! You’ll see the ‘Buy-in’ button next to your name, click on that. 7. A message will pop up asking you to sign a transaction, sign that and once the transaction is settled on the blockchain you’ll get a pop-up. If you’re on mobile or have ad blockers you probably won’t get it, simply hit refresh after 30 seconds or so. If the ‘buy-in’ button is no longer by your name, you’re all set!
